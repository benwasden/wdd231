Welcome to PC Power!

Here at PC Power, we're committed to helping you get the confidence you need to successfully build your next PC! Here, you'll find guides and suggestions to make sure you can get the right PC for you.

Want to game on your PC? How about edit video? Maybe you're more into programming and want something fast for your work. Whatever the reason, we welcome you!

Why Build My Own PC?

There are many reasons to build your own PC! When you're building your own computer, you know exactly what's going into it. You can make a system tailor-made just for you, no extra stuff required. You can get exactly what you want, and you'll know how to fix it if something goes wrong!
