Site Purpose

This site is a one-stop shop to help first time PC builders by providing parts recommendations and a guide on how to build a PC.

There will be a step by step guide for how to put together a pc, how to upgrade specific parts, and common troubleshooting issues.

There will also be a form to get personalized recommendations for them based on usecase and budget.

Scenarios

  • What is the best PC I can build for under $1000?
  • What parts would work best if I mainly use my computer for developing, video editing, etc?
  • How do I know how much system memory I need?
  • Should I buy used components? What are things I should look for when I do?
