Discover Petaluma!

Statistics

Population: ~58,500

Average Income: $51,327

Average Age: 43

Home Ownership: 63%

World Population Review

Events

  • April Every Year - Butter and Eggs Day: Join for a day of events downtown, including the famous Butter and Eggs day Parade!
  • December 6, 2024 - Tree Lighting Celebration: Join an evening of fun and welcome in the Holidays with the tree lighting event at Theater Square!
  • Veterans Day Every Year - Veterans Day Parade: Join us in honoring and remembering our Veterans each year. Musical performances and acknowledgements will occur at the gazebo prior to the parade.

Attractions

  • Helen Putnam Park - Beautiful vistas, great scenery, and outstanding walks
  • Fairgrounds - County Fairs held here regularly, along with many other events
  • Point Reyes - 20 miles out of town for some of the best hiking in the country

History

Charter Year: 1858

Renowned for: Dairy, eggs, art, and more

Significant Events:

  • Known for trade especially because of its placement on the river
  • Very prominent town during the Gold Rush
  • Where the movie "American Graffiti" was filmed
